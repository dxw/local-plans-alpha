Site allocation local plan

Last update

The Site Allocation DPD will allocate land for development needs identified in the Local Plan Review such as housing, employment and gypsy and traveller sites and review Settlement Boundaries in line with the Local Plan Review. It covers those parts of the Plan area where local communities have not chosen to identify sites through neighbourhood plans.
